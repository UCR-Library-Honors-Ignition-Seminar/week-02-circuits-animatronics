# Week 2 Assignment: Logic, Circuits & Animatronics

**Due:** End of Week 2 | **Late policy:** 20% deduction; accepted through end of Week 3

---

## Project

Build a working **servo-controlled animatronic eye** with an Arduino.

### Requirements
- Servo motor connected to the animatronic eye assembly
- Arduino code that moves the eye to at least 3 positions
- Everything wired and functioning

### Documentation
- A **short video (15-30 sec)** or **3+ photos** showing the eye in motion
- A **circuit diagram** (Tinkercad screenshot or hand-drawn photo)

---

## Reflection

Write a **~300-word reflection** using the [reflection template](reflection-template.md).

Pay special attention to Section 3 (Troubleshooting Log) — circuits almost always need debugging.
